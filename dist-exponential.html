Exponential Distribution

In probability theory and statistics, the exponential distribution is the continuous analogue of the geometric distribution, and it has the key property of being memoryless. The exponential distribution is the probability distribution of the time between events in a Poisson point process, i.e., a process in which events occur continuously and independently at a constant average rate.
